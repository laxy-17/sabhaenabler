## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (Rotate Daily)
1. **12 Rasis** - Zodiac sign personality traits
2. **27 Nakshatras** - Birth star characteristics
3. **Daily Transits** - Today's cosmic energy
4. **Do's & Don'ts** - Auspicious timing, remedies
5. **Quick Tips** - 10-second Vedic hacks
6. **Tamil Calendar** - Vratham days, festivals

### Video Format
- **Length:** 10-30 seconds
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds (attention-grabbing)
- **Body:** 15-25 seconds (3 key points max)
- **CTA:** Last 3 seconds (follow/save/share)

### Posting Schedule (IST)
- **7 AM:** Daily transit updates
- **6 PM:** Rasi/Nakshatra series (alternate days)
- **8 AM (Mon/Wed/Fri):** Quick tips
